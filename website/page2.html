Data Origin

The BigEarthNet dataset consists of 590,326 GeoTIFF images that depict birds-eye views of plots of land. The satellite that produced these images was a Sentinel-2 satellite. The images were taken between June 2017 and May 2018 and over Austria, Belgium, Finland, Ireland, Kosovo, Lithuania, Luxembourg, Portugal, Serbia, and Switzerland. The images are non-overlapping and were labeled using the multiclass labels provided by the 2018 CORINE Land Cover database. Data Features

  • 590,326 GeoTIFF Images
  • Each Image Contains 12 Bands
    • B01: Coastal Aerosal
    • B02: Blue
    • B03: Green
    • B04: Red
    • B05: Vegetation Red Edge
    • B06: Vegetation Red Edge
    • B07: Vegetation Red Edge
    • B08: Near Infrared Reflectance
    • B09: Water Vapor
    • B11: Short Wave Infrared
    • B12: Short Wave Infrared
    • B8A: Narrow Near Infrared Reflectance
  • Every Band is its own TIFF file
  • Not every band is at the same resolution
    • 60m: Coastal Aerosal (B01), Water Vapor (B09)
    • 20m: Vegetation Red Edge (B05, B06, B07), Short Wave Infrared (B11, B12), Narrow Near Infrared (B8A)
    • 10m: Blue (B02), Green (B03), Red (B04), Near Infrared Reflectance (B08)
